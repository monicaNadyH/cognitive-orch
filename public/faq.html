What payment plans are available to pay my bills?
 
 
Depending on your payment history, you may be eligible to select from the following payment plans:
a. Pay-In-Full: You pay the full policy premium on or before the policy's effective date.
b. Pay-In-Installments: You make payments in regular installments. Each installment payment plan requires an initial payment followed by an equal number of payments based on the term of your policy.

Can I set up automatic payments?
 
 
Automatic payments can be initiated using EFT or credit card.
Just follow these easy steps:

a. Login to our web site
b. Sign up for Recurring Payments
c. Choose your automatic payment method
d. Choose a schedule for automatic payments

Once you've set up an automatic payment, your premium will be deducted automatically according to the payment plan you selected.

As long as you have sufficient funds in your account on the scheduled payment dates, your payments will arrive on time - every time. We'll even send you a reminder before your payment is processed!
